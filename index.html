Our AI goes to work

Our AI instantly reviews and scores every resume using proprietary algorithms built from a decade of real hiring data. It contacts candidates by SMS and email. How can I hire 100 workers fast with Scale Hire?
With Scale Hire by SorsX, you can hire 100 or more workers in minutes, not days. Simply upload your job description and any candidate resumes you have. Our agentic AI instantly evaluates, contacts and interviews candidates, delivering ranked results so you can hire at scale with confidence.
What is the process for bulk hiring with Scale Hire?
The process is effortless and automated:

Step 1: Upload your job description and resumes of your candidates.

Step 2: SorsX AI handles everything else, from outreach, screening, video interviews to ranking candidates. You receive a clear, ranked shortlist so you can make fast, informed hiring decisions.
Who can use Scale Hire?
Any business that needs to hire multiple employees quickly can use Scale Hire. Industries with frequent or seasonal hiring needs, such as logistics, retail, hospitality, healthcare, production and customer service, rely on bulk hiring to keep operations running smoothly.
What types of roles can I fill using Scale Hire?
